Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical turnaround time?
Turnaround time depends on project complexity and my current workload. Simple charts typically take 2-3 days, while complex modcharts and menu systems may take 1-2 weeks. I'll provide a specific timeframe when discussing your project.
Do you work with engines other than Psych Engine?
Currently, I specialize in Psych Engine 0.6.3 for menu coding and source code work. For charting and modcharting, I cannot work with most major FNF engines. If you have a specific engine requirement, please ask and I'll let you know if it's possible.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept PayPal as my primary payment method. Other options may be available upon request. All payments are required upfront after work ends.
Can I request revisions to my commission?
Yes! Each commission includes up to 2 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions may incur extra charges depending on the scope of changes.
Do you offer discounts for larger projects?
Yes, I offer package deals for clients who need multiple services (e.g., charting, modcharting, and menu coding) for the same project. Contact me with your full requirements for a custom quote.
